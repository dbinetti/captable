Manage your Cap Table

Cap Table is a simple application to help you manage your startup capitalization tables -- and learn how different financing structures can affect you as a founder.

The application has five main views:

Summary
This is the high-level view of the capitalization of the company.
Financing
This is a worksheet that calculates the results of a prospective financing. It is also called a proforma, and details the pre-, new-, and post-money capitalization. It handles convertible debt conversions, prorata purchases, and option-pool expansions.
Liquidation
This worksheet calculates the proceeds from a company sale. It considers all liquidation preferences, participation, conversion ratios and other basic terms related to liquidation events.
Items
Each item that constitutes the cap table (securities, investors, certificates, etc.) are available here through a REST-style interface if you want to see the raw data.
Admin
Data entry and management are handled through the standard admin back-end, which can be accessed through the login/password 'admin/admin'.
